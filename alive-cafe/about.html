About Us

Five years of feeding the same starter

Alive Cafe is family-run, plant-filled, and built slowly — one fermentation jar, one regular, one long table conversation at a time.

The story

It started with a windowsill

In 2021, Meera and Rohan turned a disused printing shop on Riverside Lane into a nine-table cafe. The first thing they brought in wasn't furniture — it was a monstera cutting and a jar of kombucha SCOBY borrowed from Meera's mother.

Everything since has grown from that instinct: keep living things around you, feed them properly, and let the room fill in slowly instead of all at once. The espresso machine has been replaced twice. The starter and the SCOBY are both original.

The original cutting, still growing
What we stand for

Three rules we don't break

Rule 01

Nothing sits longer than a week

Beans are roasted weekly, bread is baked daily, and anything left over goes to staff, not the fridge.

Rule 02

Local first, always

Produce comes from three farms within 40km. If it's not in season, it's not on the menu.

Rule 03

One table, no laptops after noon

Mornings are for working. Afternoons are for the long table and actual conversation.
